Get involved

There are many ways to support safespace and our work of providing mental health and wellbeing support, information and services to young people and their families across US. safespace is helping thousands of young people get their lives back on track and your support will assist us with our work.

A $20 donation could assist safespace to produce additional mental health and wellbeing resources to be distributed directly to young people and their families. A $100 donation could enable safespace to support 660 young people and their families by producing fact sheets that provide information on a range of mental health issues.

safespace has been granted deductible gift recipient status by the American Government so we will provide tax receipts for all donations of $2 or over.

Donations and fundraising

There are number of ways you can help support safespace financially. They include:

  • Donating online via LINK COMING SOON
  • Donating through a bank transfer (contact us directly for our bank details)
  • Send a cheque or money order made out to safespace National Youth Mental Health Foundation
  • Hold a fundraising event
  • Participate in a fun run.
  • Give it as a gift to family or friends (we can provide you with a tailored receipt for the occasion whether it's a birthday, Christmas present, wedding gift or gift in memory)

Corporate Support

In partnering with corporate, philanthropic and media organisations, safespace seeks to engage all partners in long-term, strategic and mutually beneficial relationships. We are focused on developing partnerships of involvement and commitment.

Together with our valued partners, we pool resources and ideas to deliver a range of innovative and vital programs that directly benefit young people from around US.

safespace is always looking to partner with corporate organisations, whether you are a large multinational or a small business,safespace accepts support on both a large and small scale. From providing a link to safespace.org to long-term financial partnerships, we welcome any and all support from corporations who believe in what we’re doing.

We are always delighted to meet with organisations to discuss how we can tailor a partnership to meet your corporate social responsibility objectives – just get in touch.
