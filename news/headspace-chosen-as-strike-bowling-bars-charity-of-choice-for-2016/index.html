headspace chosen as Strike Bowling Bar's Charity of Choice for 2016

19/01/2016

headspace is delighted to announce that we have been chosen as Strike Bowling Bar’s Charity of Choice for 2016. Over the next 12 months, each Strike Bowling Bar will be undertaking a number of exciting initiatives to raise awareness and funds for the headspace centres in their local communities. We are really looking forward to working with the team at Strike this year!

headspace National Youth Mental Health Foundation Ltd is funded by the Australian Government Department of Health under the Youth Mental Health Initiative Program.
